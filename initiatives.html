Pause & Breathe

When: Last Saturday of every month

Where: American Corner (National Museum, Dar es Salaam)

Cost: Free

What to Expect:

  • Group check-ins (financial, emotional, academic)
  • Guided discussion (stress management, breakups, exam anxiety)
  • Optional private 1:1 with a mental-health expert

Our flagship monthly event creates a safe space for youth to connect, share challenges, and learn practical coping strategies in a supportive environment.

Beach-Side Art Therapy

When: Quarterly

Where: White House Mikoko (Kwa Mwalimu Nyerere Beach)

Cost: 10,000 TZS

What to Expect:

  • Creative painting exercises to externalize feelings
  • Small-group reflection circles (max 30)
  • On-site 1:1 support available
  • All art supplies provided

Combining the therapeutic benefits of art with the calming environment of the beach, these quarterly sessions offer a unique opportunity to process emotions through creativity.

Next Session:

July 2025: "Healing Through Expression"

Explore how art can aid in processing grief and loss.

August 2025: "Finding Your Calm"

Painting to reduce anxiety and promote relaxation.
